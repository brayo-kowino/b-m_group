Membership Guide

A comprehensive overview of the operational framework, contribution mandates, and wealth generation protocols for exclusive members of the B&M Group.

1. Eligibility & Onboarding

B&M Group operates strictly on an invitation-only basis. Prospective members must be nominated by an existing stakeholder in good standing and pass a preliminary executive review.

  • App Activation: Upon approval, new members are issued a unique activation key to register their profile on the B&M mobile app.
  • KYC Verification: Valid national identification and primary contact details must be verified within the app before any financial capabilities are unlocked.

2. Capital Contributions

Monthly Minimums

Every member is mandated to make a strict minimum monthly deposit. These funds are pooled to generate collective lending capital.

The 5th-Day Rule

All contributions must reflect in the digital ledger by the 5th of every month. Late submissions trigger automated system penalties.

3. Borrowing Protocol

The core benefit of B&M Group is low-barrier access to capital based on a member's disciplined savings history.
