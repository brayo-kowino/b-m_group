Zero-Compromise Security

Your financial data and collective capital are protected by enterprise-grade encryption, strict access controls, and immutable ledger architecture.

End-to-End Encryption

All communications between the B&M app and our secure servers utilize advanced TLS 1.3 encryption. Your personal data, ledger balances, and loan requests are entirely shielded from unauthorized interception.

Immutable Ledger Logs

To prevent financial disputes, our database architecture ensures that once a transaction (deposit, loan approval, or penalty) is recorded, it cannot be manually altered or deleted, ensuring absolute transparency.

Strict Session Management

The app enforces automated session timeouts and requires biometric or PIN authentication for sensitive actions, ensuring that even if your device is compromised, your financial profile remains inaccessible.

Digital Guarantor Signatures

Loan underwriting requires strict digital authorization from guarantors within the app environment. This electronic handshake is logged with timestamps to prevent repudiation.

Notice on Data Privacy

B&M Group does not sell, rent, or distribute member data to third-party institutions. Internal data is strictly utilized for group liquidity calculations and administrative oversight by the Executive Committee.
