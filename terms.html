1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing the B&M Group member portal, executing transactions, or maintaining active membership within the association, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service ("Terms") and the official B&M Group Constitution. If you do not agree with any part of these terms, you must immediately formally request membership termination.

2. Private Association Status

B&M Group is a strictly private, invite-only savings and credit association. We are not a licensed public bank, microfinance institution, or public investment fund. All capital is collectively owned by vetted members and managed strictly according to internal bylaws for the mutual benefit of the group.

3. Financial Commitments & Penalties

  • Mandatory Contributions: All agreed-upon weekly/monthly savings must be deposited by the 5th day of the respective month.
  • Strict Enforcement: Failure to meet deposit deadlines will trigger automatic administrative penalties as outlined in the Constitution. "Zero Emotion" applies to all financial commitments.

4. Lending, Credit, and Default

Access to credit is a privilege, not a right, earned through disciplined saving.

  • Borrowing Limits: Members in good standing may borrow up to three times (3x) their accumulated total savings, subject to group liquidity.
  • Guarantor Liability: All loans require approved member guarantors. In the event of a default, liability is immediately and fully transferred to the guarantors. The group reserves the right to recover defaulted funds directly from the guarantors' accumulated savings.

5. Portal Access and Confidentiality

The online ledger and member portal contain highly sensitive financial data. Members must maintain absolute confidentiality regarding group operations, individual balances, and lending activities.

  • Account credentials must never be shared.
  • Members are responsible for all activity conducted under their account.
  • Any unauthorized distribution of internal group data will result in immediate expulsion and potential legal action.

6. Exit & Termination

Members may exit the group voluntarily by providing the formal notice period required by the Constitution. Upon exit, a member's net savings (total savings plus dividends, minus any outstanding loans, penalties, or guarantor liabilities) will be disbursed according to the group's liquidity schedule. Management reserves the right to expel any member for constitutional violations.

7. Governing Law

These terms and the operations of the B&M Group shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of Kenya. Any disputes arising from these terms will first be subjected to internal arbitration as dictated by the group's management committee.
