1. Why We Automate Discipline

The B&M Group operates on the core principle of Zero Emotional Interference. In traditional groups, late payments lead to arguments, favoritism, and broken relationships.

We built this portal to act as an impartial judge. The system's algorithms do not care about excuses; they only read the math. By enforcing absolute transparency and strict deadlines, we protect the group's capital and ensure fairness for every member. Discipline before access.

2. Standard Payment Protocol

The Constitution mandates a contribution of KSH 70 per week (plus KSH 10 per extra day in the month). Your primary responsibility is not just paying, but reporting the payment.

  1. Send your contribution to the official B&M Bank/M-Pesa account.
  2. Log into the Member Portal immediately.
  3. Enter the exact Amount and the M-Pesa Transaction Code.
  4. Submit for Admin verification.
The Smart Algorithm: The system automatically checks your ledger on the 7th and 21st of every month. If your cleared funds are behind schedule, you will receive automated disciplinary warnings.

3. Emergency Fallback Procedure

We understand that technology fails. If you have made your payment but cannot access the Member Portal (e.g., website maintenance, forgotten password, or lack of internet), you must use the official Offline Reporting method to avoid algorithmic penalties.

The Offline SMS/WhatsApp Format

Send a direct message to an Executive Admin (Brian or Maximilla) strictly using this exact format:

B&M PAYMENT REPORT
Name: [Your Name]
Amount: [KSH Amount]
M-Pesa Code: [e.g., SGH1234567]

* Note: The Admin will manually verify the code against the bank statement and inject the funds directly into your ledger via the Executive Dashboard. This will instantly satisfy the Smart Warning Engine.

4. Loans & Voluntary Exits

  • Borrowing Limit: Members can borrow up to 2x their personal savings, provided they have a minimum balance of KSH 500.
  • Interest: A flat rate of 15% is applied to all loans.
  • Exits (Sec 13.1): If you wish to leave the B&M Group, you must submit a formal application through the portal. Absolutely no exits will be processed if you have an active, uncleared loan.
