Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026 | Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

At B&M Group, trust and absolute transparency are the foundations of our financial ecosystem. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your personal and financial information when you use our member portal and participate in the group's activities. As a strictly private association, we prioritize the confidentiality of our members' capital.

2. Information We Collect

To maintain an accurate ledger and ensure the security of group funds, we collect the following data upon your admission and throughout your membership:

  • Identity Verification: Full legal name, National ID/Passport number, and secure login credentials.
  • Contact Information: Active email addresses and mobile phone numbers.
  • Financial Data: Transaction histories, M-Pesa/bank account details used for deposits and disbursements, loan application details, and dividend accruals.

3. How We Use Your Data

We operate with a "Zero Emotion" policy, meaning your data is used strictly for objective financial administration. We use your information to:

  • Maintain your personal financial ledger and track weekly/monthly contributions.
  • Process loan applications, assess borrowing eligibility, and calculate compounding interest.
  • Distribute annual dividends based on your precise contribution weight.
  • Enforce administrative penalties for late submissions or defaults.

4. Data Visibility & Guarantors

B&M Group does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties or marketing agencies. However, due to the nature of our collective capital risk, internal visibility rules apply:

  • Your total savings and loan status are accessible to the Management Committee (Chairperson, Treasurer, and Secretary) for operational purposes.
  • Guarantor Transparency: If you apply for a loan, your guarantors have the right to know your current financial standing within the group. In the event of a default, your full loan profile and penalty status will be disclosed to your guarantors as they assume the financial liability.

5. Data Security

We implement strict digital security measures to protect your data within the member portal. Passwords are encrypted, and access to the master ledger is heavily restricted. Members are required to keep their portal access credentials strictly confidential. Management will never ask for your portal password.

6. Your Rights & Data Retention

You have the right to access your complete financial ledger 24/7 via the member portal. If you identify any discrepancies, you may formally request a ledger review from the Treasurer. Your data will be retained for the active duration of your membership. Upon formal exit from the B&M Group and the settling of all financial obligations, your data will be securely archived for historical record-keeping purposes as required by group policy.

Questions Regarding Your Data?

If you have any questions about how your data is handled or need to update your contact information, please reach out directly to the B&M Group Secretary.
