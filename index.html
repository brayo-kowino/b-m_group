Governance

Key Group Policies

The uncompromising operational rules that protect our collective capital.

View Full Constitution

Savings & Contributions

Minimum monthly contributions must be deposited by the 5th of every month. Late submissions trigger automatic administrative penalties.

Lending Limits

Members can borrow up to 2x their accumulated savings, subject to guarantor approval and current group liquidity reserves.

Penalties & Defaults

Defaulting on loan repayments transfers the liability directly to the guarantors. Strict interest compounding applies to late payments.

The Architecture

Founded on Trust. Managed by Experience.

The B&M Group wasn't started in a corporate boardroom. It was founded in early 2026 by a husband and wife team who decided to make a tangible difference in their community's financial habits.

Tired of traditional banking barriers and watching friends struggle to access fair credit, they combined their expertise in administration and finance to build a zero-emotion, rules-based financial ecosystem. Today, they serve as the collective management team, ensuring every penny is accounted for and every policy is strictly enforced.

Zero Emotion. Absolute Accountability.
