Institutional Policies

The uncompromising operational framework designed to protect our collective capital.

View Full Constitution

Capital Contributions

Minimum monthly contributions must clear by the 5th of every month. Late submissions automatically trigger non-negotiable administrative penalties.

Lending Parameters

Members may leverage up to 2x their accumulated ledger balance, strictly subject to guarantor authorization and total group liquidity reserves.

Default Protocols

Defaulting on loan amortization schedules transfers the liability directly to the guarantors. Aggressive interest compounding is applied to all accounts in arrears.
