What We Do

Save Together, Grow Together

B&M Group is a private savings and lending platform. We follow strict rules, keep careful records, and use a mobile app to help members save money and borrow when needed.

When you join, your savings combine with other members to create a fund. You can borrow from this fund, and you also earn money back from loans made by the group.

Safe and Secure

Your money records are protected with strong security in the app.

Governance

Institutional Policies

The uncompromising operational framework designed to protect our collective capital.

View Full Constitution

Monthly Savings

All members must save money each month by the 5th. If you save late through the app, you will pay an extra fee.

Borrowing Rules

You can borrow up to twice the amount you have saved. A group member must approve your loan request through the app.

Missing Loan Payments

If you don't pay your loan on time, the person who approved it must pay instead. Extra charges will keep growing on accounts that are late.
