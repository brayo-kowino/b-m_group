Governance

Institutional Policies

The uncompromising operational framework designed to protect our collective capital.

View Full Constitution

Capital Contributions

Minimum monthly contributions must clear by the 5th of every month. Late submissions via the platform trigger automated administrative penalties.

Lending Parameters

Members may leverage up to 2x their accumulated ledger balance, subject to in-app digital guarantor authorization and group liquidity reserves.

Default Protocols

Defaulting on loan amortization schedules transfers the liability directly to guarantors. Aggressive interest compounding is enforced by system algorithms on all accounts in arrears.
